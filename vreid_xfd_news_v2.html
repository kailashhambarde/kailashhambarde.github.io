The VReID-XFD Workshop at WACV 2026 is a Wrap

The 1st Workshop on Video-based Human Recognition at Extreme Far Distances successfully concluded at WACV 2026 — organised by Kailash A. Hambarde and Hugo Proença from Instituto de Telecomunicações & UBI, Portugal. Papers are now openly available.

✦

The VReID-XFD workshop brought together researchers tackling one of computer vision's most demanding open problems: reliably identifying people from video captured at extreme distances, under degraded conditions, across clothing changes and challenging UAV viewpoints.

The half-day programme at WACV 2026 paired an archival paper track with the DetReIDX Challenge — a competition with hidden-label evaluation that attracted teams presenting a diverse set of approaches, from scale-adaptive re-identification frameworks to CLIP-guided aerial-ground matching and super-resolution pipelines. All papers are now freely accessible via IEEE Xplore and CVF Open Access.
